Before you book

Is the consultation actually free?

Yes — the first consultation is free of charge. You only pay if you proceed with further diagnostics or treatment, and that's always discussed with you upfront.

Do I need an X-ray or MRI first?

No. Bring any existing reports if you have them, but it's not required — your specialist can recommend the right diagnostics during the consultation if needed.

Can I book without insurance?

Yes, consultations are available with or without insurance. Our coordinator will walk you through costs during the confirmation call.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem — just call or WhatsApp the clinic directly using the number from your confirmation, anytime before your slot.
